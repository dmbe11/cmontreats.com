C'MoN is a family owned business created with the desire to provide high quality treats for our family pets. We own four dogs, a Weimaraner and three Great Danes named Charley, Molly, Natalya, and Madeline. Molly has always been a healthy and vibrant dog but when she was still a puppy we discovered when she was given high processed treats, she would have an adverse reaction. In the worst cases she would have severe stomach cramps and show she was in pain.

We quickly realized the treats were to blame and started looking for an alternative. After doing research, I discovered that feeding single ingredient treats might stop the problem. So, after testing a few options we discovered freeze dried single ingredient treats.  These treats did not cause the issues  but I found the quality to be inconsistent and in my opinion they were expensive. We thought for what we were paying for these treats we should either get more or  higher quality. Then we realized that most of the options were from organ meat, which was really difficult to tell about the quality and thought it would be better to start with high quality food  that had information regarding where the meat came from..

After all of this we thought, why don't we make our own and if we could maybe sell them on a small scale.
